Proprietary Index

Disruption Severity Index (DSI)

Our composite 0–100 score that objectively measures weather impact on gig work — powering every auto-payout.

DSI = w₁·Temp + w₂·Rain + w₃·Wind + w₄·AQI + w₅·Visibility
Temperature Impact (w₁) 35%
Rainfall Intensity (w₂) 25%
Wind Speed (w₃) 20%
Air Quality Index (w₄) 12%
Visibility Score (w₅) 8%
🟢

DSI 0–19 — Normal

No disruption. Business as usual for workers.

🟡

DSI 20–39 — Watch

Mild conditions. Workers alerted via WhatsApp.

🟠

DSI 40–59 — Moderate

Auto-payout triggered: 25% of coverage limit.

🔴

DSI 60–79 — Severe

Escalated payout: 50–75% of coverage limit.

🟣

DSI 80–89 — Extreme

Maximum payout: 100% of coverage limit.

⚫

DSI 90–100 — Catastrophic

Emergency mode: Max payout + safety advisories.
